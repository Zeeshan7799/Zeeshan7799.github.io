PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NoBreak-X-Days collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the mobile application.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We may collect the following categories of information:

1.1 Information you create in the app

  • Habit data, including habit names, schedules, colors, icons, start and end dates, streak data, archive and pause status, and related settings.
  • Completion data, including completion dates, status, and optional notes.
  • Reward and progress information related to milestones and unlock states.
  • Reminder times and certain app preferences.

1.2 Account information

If you choose to sign in with Google, we may collect and process account-related information made available through Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile photo
  • Your account identifier or user ID

1.3 Diagnostic and technical information

To help maintain app reliability, we may collect technical diagnostics and crash-related information through Firebase Crashlytics, such as:

  • Crash logs
  • Error reports
  • Device and app version information
  • Basic diagnostic metadata needed to investigate failures

1.4 Notification-related information

If you enable reminders, the app stores notification preferences such as reminder time settings and notification permission status.

1.5 Locally stored drawing or image references

If you create manual completion drawings or similar local content, related local file references may be stored on your device. These local references are not part of the app's current cloud sync payload.

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the habit tracking, streak, reminder, reward, and settings features of the app
  • Sync your data across sessions and devices when you sign in
  • Authenticate your account
  • Send local reminder notifications that you choose to enable
  • Export your data to a local JSON file when you request an export
  • Maintain app security, stability, and performance
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and errors
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable

3. HOW DATA IS STORED

3.1 On your device

The app stores much of your information locally on your device using local database and preference storage. This includes habit data, completion records, app settings, reminder preferences, and certain reward or UI state information.

3.2 In the cloud

If you sign in, the app may sync certain data to backend services associated with the app. Based on the current implementation, this may include:

  • Habit records
  • Completion records and optional notes
  • Certain settings and reward-related state
  • Reminder-related sync data

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app uses third-party services to operate key features:

4.1 Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication

Used to sign you in and manage authenticated sessions.

4.2 Firebase Crashlytics

Used to collect crash and reliability information.

4.3 Firebase-hosted backend services

Used for account-linked syncing and server-side data operations.

These third-party providers may process information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. NOTIFICATIONS

The app uses local notifications to remind you about habits and streak-related activity. These are generated on your device. The app does not currently implement remote marketing push notifications.

To support reminders, the app may request notification-related permissions and, on some Android devices, exact alarm and reboot-related permissions so reminders can be scheduled and restored reliably.

6. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers and infrastructure providers needed to operate the app, such as authentication, backend, and crash reporting services
  • When you choose to sign in and sync data
  • When you choose to export or share your exported file
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security

The app is not currently designed to serve third-party advertising.

7. EXPORTS

If you use the export feature, the app generates a JSON export file on your device that may contain your habit and completion information, including notes. You control what you do with that exported file. If you share or upload it yourself, that sharing is your responsibility.

8. DATA RETENTION

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the app's features and for legitimate operational purposes.

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app.
  • If you use account-based sync, server-side data may remain associated with your account until it is deleted.

9. ACCOUNT DELETION AND YOUR CHOICES

If you use account-based features, the app includes an account deletion flow. When you request account deletion, the app sends a request to remove your account and associated server-side data.

You may also choose to:

  • Use the app without signing in, subject to available functionality
  • Disable notifications in your device or app settings
  • Delete local app data from your device
  • Contact us with questions or requests about your data

10. SECURITY

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The app is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to consent to data processing on their own. If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of applicable law, contact us so we can review the issue.

12. INTERNATIONAL DATA PROCESSING

If you use cloud-based features, your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own, depending on the infrastructure used by our service providers.

13. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by a new Effective Date. Continued use of the app after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.
